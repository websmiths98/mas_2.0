Our services are structured to align with different operational needs, offering scalable logistics models that adapt to your business requirements.

2PL (Second-Party Logistics)
Focused on transportation and basic logistics support between supplier and recipient.

3PL (Third-Party Logistics)
Covers freight forwarding, shipping, and coordination of logistics activities across the supply chain.

4PL (Fourth-Party Logistics)
A fully integrated logistics model where we manage end-to-end supply chain operations, including planning, execution, and coordination.

==================== SPECIALIZED SERVICES ====================

🔹 CUSTOMS BROKERAGE

Our highly efficient customs brokerage, clearance and compliance service is designed to take the complexity out of the customs process.

We understand the importance of customs brokerage in getting your products to their destination. When moving freight across international borders, we work to minimise risks by carefully managing the smooth transition of cargo from country to country. We employ dedicated professionals around the world who have multi-lingual capability and a clear understanding of the intricacies of trade regulations and agreements at global, regional, national and local levels.

To ensure the complete security of your goods, even in challenging environments, our personnel serve as the first point of contact with both customs authorities and receiving customers. This allows us to provide reassuring guidance and expert advice on matters relating to customs.

When it comes to moving your products across international borders, MAS Logistics provides you with a smooth and hassle-free experience.
